Architecture

Containerized. Configurable. Consistent.

Every XLeratorAI accelerator is delivered as a containerized application with independently configurable layers. The same container image runs identically whether in our cloud, your cloud, or your data center — giving your organization the flexibility to change its hosting, AI, or data strategy without rebuilding the product.

Deployment Models

We deploy where your data lives.

From fully managed SaaS to complete air-gapped isolation, XLeratorAI supports the deployment model that fits your security posture, regulatory environment, and budget. Every model delivers the same accelerator — the difference is where it runs and who manages the infrastructure.

☁️
Fastest Start

XLeratorAI Managed Cloud

We host and operate your instance in our secure cloud environment. Zero infrastructure burden on your team — fully managed updates, monitoring, and backups. Ideal for teams that want to move fast without provisioning infrastructure.

  • Zero infrastructure overhead
  • Fully managed updates, monitoring, and backups
  • Fastest path to production
  • Lowest upfront investment
🏢
Your Cloud

Customer Private Cloud

Deployed into your own Azure, AWS, or GCP subscription. Your network, your firewall rules, your compliance boundary. We provide the container images and deployment automation — you retain full control.

  • Runs in your cloud subscription
  • Your network and firewall rules
  • Container images + IaC templates provided
  • Leverages existing enterprise agreements
🔒
Air-Gapped

On-Premises

For organizations with strict data residency or regulatory requirements. XLeratorAI runs entirely within your data center with no external dependencies. Supports classified and regulated environments.

  • Runs entirely in your data center
  • No external network dependencies
  • Full data sovereignty compliance
  • Supports zero-trust environments
🔀
Flexible

Hybrid

Keep sensitive data on-prem while leveraging cloud AI services through secure, encrypted channels. Designed for organizations transitioning to cloud at their own pace.

  • Sensitive data stays on-premises
  • Cloud AI through encrypted channels
  • Transition at your own pace
  • Best of both worlds
AI / LLM Layer

Choose how AI processes your data.

The AI layer handles all inference and generation. Every option delivers the same accelerator functionality — the difference is where the model runs and who manages it. Your data is never used to train, fine-tune, or improve third-party AI models.

☁️
Default

Cloud API

Enterprise-grade AI APIs managed by XLeratorAI. Fastest to deploy with standard data processing agreements. All AI interactions are logged and auditable.

  • Fastest deployment — days, not weeks
  • Managed and maintained by XLeratorAI
  • Standard DPA included
  • Full AI audit trail
🏢
Enterprise

Enterprise AI Platform

Azure AI Foundry or equivalent. AI runs within your cloud tenant — data never leaves your security boundary. Model versioning ensures reproducibility of results.

  • Data stays within your cloud tenant
  • Leverages existing enterprise agreements
  • Meets data residency requirements
  • Model versioning for reproducibility
🔒
Air-Gapped

Local / Private Models

Ollama or similar local model runners. Complete air-gapped operation — zero data leaves the network. AI outputs are labeled and traceable to source inputs.

  • Complete air-gapped operation
  • Zero data leaves the network
  • Full control over model selection
  • Classified environment support
Data / Storage Layer

Choose what persists — and where.

The data layer is completely independent from the AI layer. You have full control over what data is stored, where it lives, and who has access. All stored data is encrypted at rest with AES-256, including backups and uploaded documents.

⚡

Stateless Operation

Data processed in-memory only. Nothing persists after the session ends. Ideal for evaluations, sensitive ad-hoc analysis, or organizations that prohibit data retention.

  • Zero data retention
  • Session-scoped processing only
  • No database provisioning required
  • Simplest compliance posture
🗃️

Client-Managed Storage

SQLite on client infrastructure or cloud object storage (AWS S3, Azure Blob). Your organization owns, controls, and has full visibility into all stored data.

  • Client owns and controls all data
  • SQLite, AWS S3, or Azure Blob
  • Full audit trail and access logging
  • Private endpoints — no public exposure
📊

Dedicated Data Warehouse

XLeratorAI provides the schema and data model; your organization provisions the database. Enables structured analytics, trending, and cross-accelerator reporting.

  • XLeratorAI-designed schema
  • Client-provisioned infrastructure
  • Automated backups with point-in-time recovery
  • Historical trending and benchmarking
