Healthcare
Strategy Accelerator

Regional Healthcare Provider

A 3,000-employee regional health system had not updated their strategic plan in over two years. Leadership knew their project portfolio was misaligned to organizational goals but couldn't articulate which projects to cut, which to accelerate, and what was missing. The strategy planning process had historically taken 6–8 months of executive working sessions and consultant involvement.

Challenge

Outdated strategy, unclear portfolio prioritization, and no bandwidth to run a multi-month planning process without disrupting operations.

Solution Delivered

AI Strategy Accelerator — configured for healthcare industry with competitive intelligence, strategic plan generation, and portfolio scoring.

Time to Value

First working session to board-ready strategic plan: 4 business days.

Key Outcome

18-month strategy generated, 14 projects scored and ranked, 3 low-alignment projects recommended for deprioritization.

2 days From blank page to completed strategic plan — vs. the prior 6-month process.
Financial Services
PMO Accelerator

Mid-Market Financial Services Firm

A financial services firm managing 50+ concurrent technology projects was drowning in manual reporting. The PMO team spent an estimated 12–15 hours per week compiling status reports, chasing down project managers, and reformatting data for leadership decks. Critical issues were regularly missed until they became escalations.

Challenge

Manual, error-prone PMO reporting with no real-time visibility into project health, cost variance, or emerging risks.

Solution Delivered

AI PMO Accelerator — connected to existing project data sources with automated health scoring, cost variance alerts, and executive dashboards.

Time to Value

Working prototype in 18 days. Full deployment in 30 days from first session.

Key Outcome

PMO reporting time eliminated from 12+ hrs/week to near-zero. Two at-risk projects surfaced and corrected before they became escalations.

40% Overall reduction in manual reporting burden across the PMO organization.
Manufacturing
Custom AI Workflow

National Manufacturing Company

A national manufacturer with 12 regional divisions was struggling with a slow, paper-heavy capital project intake and approval process. Projects would sit in queues for 3–4 weeks before receiving initial approval — causing downstream delays, budget timing issues, and significant frustration across division leadership teams.

Challenge

Slow intake and approval process averaging 3–4 weeks per project, with no visibility into queue status or bottleneck locations.

Solution Delivered

Custom AI workflow for capital project intake — automated routing, AI-assisted form review, approval tracking, and status visibility.

Time to Value

Full cycle-time improvement realized within 60 days of deployment.

Key Outcome

Average intake cycle time reduced from 3.5 weeks to 8 days. Cross-department visibility improved and manual routing eliminated entirely.

3× Faster capital project intake and approval cycle time — from weeks to days.
